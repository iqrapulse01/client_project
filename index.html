WELCOME TO FOOD QUEST!

AN EXCITING CULINARY ADVENTURE:

Food Quest is an innovative AR/QR scavenger hunt designed to take your dining experience to the next level. Imagine exploring your favorite city, discovering hidden culinary gems, and winning free meals and exclusive rewards along the way. Whether you're a foodie looking for your next great meal or an adventurer seeking a fun and interactive experience, Food Quest offers something for everyone.

HOW IT WORKS:

Food Quest combines the thrill of a scavenger hunt with the joy of discovering new restaurants. Players use their smartphones to scan QR codes at participating restaurants, unlocking clues and challenges that guide them to their next destination. Each location offers a unique culinary experience, from tasting signature dishes to meeting the chefs behind the creations. As you progress through the hunt, you'll collect points and earn rewards that can be redeemed for free meals, discounts, and other exciting prizes.


AUGMENTED REALITY EXPERIENCE:

Our AR technology brings the scavenger hunt to life, creating an immersive experience that engages all your senses. Visualize hidden clues, interact with virtual characters, and uncover secrets about each restaurant's history and specialties. The AR elements add a layer of excitement and intrigue, making each visit more than just a meal—it's an adventure.

COMMUNITY AND COLLABORATION:

Food Quest is not just about the hunt; it's about building a community of food enthusiasts. Share your experiences on social media, compete with friends, and join a network of fellow adventurers. Restaurants also benefit from increased visibility and customer engagement, creating a win-win situation for everyone involved. By participating in Food Quest, you're supporting local businesses and discovering new favorites along the way.

JOIN THE FUN:

Ready to embark on your culinary adventure? Sign up for Food Quest today and start exploring the best dining spots in your city. Whether you're a solo explorer or part of a team, Food Quest promises an unforgettable experience filled with delicious discoveries and exciting rewards. Don't miss out on the chance to turn your dining routine into a thrilling quest!
